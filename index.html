What is RareBus?
A dumb little game for people who are weirdly into public transit.

You collect strange, obscure, and cursed bus/train routes by clicking Hop On.
Rarer routes give you a higher Unhinged Score.
That's the whole point. No deeper meaning. Just collect weird routes.
